💡 How the Resell Credit Works

When we show up, we sort everything in your garage before loading the truck. Items with resale value — tools, electronics, furniture, sporting goods — get pulled and sold through our resale channels. We credit a portion of what we recover directly back to your final invoice.

Example: Your Reset Standard job is $499. We find $120 worth of sellable items. We credit $60 back. Your final bill: $439.

You don't lift a finger. We do the work. You pay less.
